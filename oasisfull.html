OASIS RESORT

Studio Project

  • Browse Images

Concept Development

The central core of the resort which happens to be the courtyard acts as a direct analogy to the oasis with the other spaces of resort placed functionally around it as if the oasis is leading to genesis of a completely new civilisation. Borrowing elements from the vernacular haveli style of architecture of which courtyard is an integral part, stack effect happens in the best way possible.

The community interaction deck serves as the connector of major spaces of the resort and also acts as a gathering point for everyone to unite. The cottages have been strategically placed for the best view possible and forms an arrangement with the other constituents of the resort which elevates the overall life energy of the space and tries to give it's occupants a surreal experience.

The best intelligence test is what we do with our leisure.
