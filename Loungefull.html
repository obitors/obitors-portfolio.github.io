FAMILY LOUNGE

Studio Project

  • Browse Images

Concept Development

A family room is generally an informal, all-purpose space in a house. The family room is designed to be a place where family and guests gather for group recreation like talking, reading, watching TV, and other family activities. When it comes to beautiful interior design, often times less is more and simple equates to elegance. With a clean and simple palate, the right accessories can help add some pop and pizazz to most any space. Minimal furniture, natural wood tones and white linens make this bedroom feel simple and sunny. Again, consider designs that are simple, yet functional. The natural wood tones actually help the other design elements to stand out and shine. Holistic Design Approach

Everything is an expression of God. You, me and what we create. Finding the sweet spot where what you do naturally is in service to helping another live their best life is the shift that is happening now. Holistic interior design is not only about design the spaces for your clients, but finding a holistic design solution for yourself and business that support your life's purpose.
